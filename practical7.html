Doraemon

The Robot Cat from the Future

About Doraemon

Doraemon is a famous Japanese cartoon character created by Fujiko F. Fujio. He is a robotic cat sent from the future to help a boy named Nobita Nobi improve his life.

Doraemon has a magical pocket from which he takes out futuristic gadgets to solve problems, but often things go wrong in funny ways.

Main Characters

  • Doraemon – Robot cat with amazing gadgets
  • Nobita – Lazy but kind-hearted boy
  • Shizuka – Intelligent and caring friend
  • Gian – Strong and sometimes rude boy
  • Suneo – Clever and boastful friend

Fun Facts

  • Doraemon was sent from the 22nd century
  • He loves eating Dorayaki (a Japanese sweet)
  • His pocket contains unlimited futuristic gadgets
  • The show teaches friendship and problem-solving
